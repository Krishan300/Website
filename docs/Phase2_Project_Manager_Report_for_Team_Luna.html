Project-Wide

How well did your team estimate effort?

After the inital assignment, we met as a team and all agreed that this phase will require a very large time commitment. Rather than overestimate our own coding abilities this time around, we decided that it would be better to just meet and communicate much more frequently. This allowed us more accurate effort estimates because we were estimating much smaller tasks. Because of this, our effort level estimates were fairly accurate.

What techniques (team programming, timelines, group design exercises) did you use to mitigate risk?

We used team programming extensively throughout this phase. We scheduled meetings at least 3 times a week and would work as closely as we could without having any one person do all of the work. As for timelines, we found it easier without them. Most timelines we set we ignored in the last phase, so we did away with them completely. For design, we tried to remain flexible and allowed the developers to deviate if they ran into any issues.
